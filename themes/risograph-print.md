Two-color risograph print on uncoated paper stock, slight registration misalignment between the two layers, visible halftone dot texture, flat graphic shapes with no gradient shading, limited palette of a deep ink color and a warm fluorescent accent (e.g., navy + fluorescent pink, or forest green + burnt orange). Bold simplified forms, slight ink saturation variation across the print, faint paper grain visible. No photographic realism, no smooth gradients, no glossy rendering, no text overlays, no watermarks.
