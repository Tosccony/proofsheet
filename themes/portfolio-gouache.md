Hand-painted gouache illustration on textured cream paper, visible directional brushstrokes and confident-but-slightly-imperfect linework, observational Maira-Kalman editorial sensibility — quiet, intimate, contemplative mood. Muted earthy palette limited to about five colors per image (warm off-whites, dusty rose, brick red, sage green, mustard yellow, soft denim blue). Spare composition with a single subject placed slightly off-center, generous negative space, naïve-but-considered perspective without strict vanishing points. No photorealistic rendering, no 3D shading, no slick digital vector look, no glossy gradients — gouache opacity and paper texture should remain visible. No text overlays, no signatures, no recognizable brand names, no watermarks.
