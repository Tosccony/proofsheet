Sharp modern digital photography, bright even lighting from large softboxes producing minimal shadow drama, neutral palette of warm whites, light grays, and one restrained accent color (often deep navy, slate, or warm beige). Crisp focus throughout, subtle depth-of-field for separation but nothing dramatic. Composition is clean and balanced — subject placed deliberately with calm negative space around it. Modern minimal aesthetic, contemporary commercial photography sensibility. Approachable but professional mood. No dramatic low-key shadows, no high-saturation primary colors, no vintage film effects, no painterly stylization, no text overlays, no recognizable brand names or logos, no watermarks.
