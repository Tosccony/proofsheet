Charcoal on textured cream paper, loose confident gesture lines defining the subject with several visible underdrawing marks left in place, soft smudged shading rubbed with a finger or stump, varying line weight from delicate hair-thin contours to bold dark accents. Monochrome — black charcoal against the warm off-white paper tone, occasional white-chalk highlights on prominent forms. Loose unfinished sketchbook feel rather than a polished rendered drawing. Slightly off-center composition. Quiet thoughtful mood. No digital smoothness, no photographic detail, no color, no text overlays, no signature, no watermarks.
